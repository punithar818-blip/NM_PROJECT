Branding for 'Eco-Coffee'

A full brand identity and packaging project for a sustainable coffee brand. The goal was to create a natural, organic feel that resonated with environmentally conscious consumers.

Challenge: Design a logo and a cohesive visual language that communicated sustainability without sacrificing a modern, clean aesthetic.

Solution: We developed a minimalist logomark inspired by coffee beans and nature, paired with a warm, earthy color palette. The packaging used recycled materials with simple, elegant typography.
